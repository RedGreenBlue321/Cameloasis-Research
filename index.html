what we do
Statistical & Data Science Services

Our team of experts specializes in providing statistical and data science services, focusing on delivering comprehensive quantitative research solutions. Whether you're exploring market trends, measuring policy impacts, or analyzing complex social behaviors, we equip organizations with the insights needed to make informed, data-driven decisions. At DeltaOasis Research, we combine rigorous methodology with innovative analytics to turn your data challenges into opportunities for growth and understanding.

DeltaOasis Research Core Services - Quantitative Research

Consulting

We provide expert statistical consulting tailored to your research needs, whether you are in academia, government, or private sectors. Our consulting services help you design robust studies, choose appropriate methodologies, and interpret complex data effectively to drive evidence-based decisions

Collaboration

We provide collaborative research services that integrate our statistical expertise with your domain knowledge to tackle specific social science challenges. This approach allows for co-developing research designs, conducting joint analyses, and co-authoring papers or reports that advance knowledge and inform policy.

Traning

We provide specialized training workshops and webinars focused on quantitative methods and the latest statistical software tools. Our training is designed to empower your team with the skills needed to execute high-quality research independently, enhancing their capabilities in data analysis and interpretation.

Who We Serve

At DeltaOasis Research, we serve a diverse clientele spanning multiple disciplines within the social sciences. Research Institutes

Financial services firms succeed when they foresee risks and opportunities before they are priced into the market. Superforecasting helps these firms make smart bets on consumer trends, regulatory developments, and geopolitical shifts that the less-informed crowd has yet to recognize. See how our Covid vaccine Superforecasts anticipated financial markets.

Energy

Energy companies rely on forecasts to decide which resources to develop, which markets to serve, and what prices to charge. Good Judgment helps energy providers quantify geopolitical and regulatory risks so that the firms can build decision models that take account of more than just engineering and economic data. Read about a Wharton collaboration with Good Judgment to forecast electric-vehicle innovations.

Government

Policymakers and military leaders leverage Superforecasting to anticipate events of global consequence more rapidly and precisely. Early, actionable forecasts allow strategists to “get it righter, faster.” Our partnership with ForgeFront makes it easy for UK government clients to access Superforecasting tools. Learn how the US State Department used Superforecasts to inform efforts to eradicate illegal coca crops.

NGOs & Nonprofits

NGOs and nonprofits seeking to make the most of limited resources must forecast where the need will be greatest. Superforecasting accelerates insights on public health and humanitarian crises, so that our clients can deliver timely aid where it’s most needed. Discover how adelphi worked with Good Judgment to anticipate climate-related risks.
